THE SAUCE

Two Ways to Play

Medium

SMOOTH • TANGY • VERSATILE

The everyday go-to. Rich, smoky sweetness with a Carolina tang that plays well with ribs, chicken, pulled pork — or anything you put it on. Bold enough to stand up, smooth enough to pour on everything.

Spicy

BOLD • KICK • ADDICTIVE

Same backbone, more bite. The spicy brings real heat without burning out the flavor. It builds slow and finishes strong. For people who think medium is just the warm-up.
